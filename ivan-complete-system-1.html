The SQ3R Method
Best Reading Technique
Survey, Question, Read, Recite, Review. Used by top university students worldwide...
S — Survey: Skim all headings in 2 minutes before reading. Get the big picture first.
Q — Question: Turn each heading into a question. Now you read to find an answer.
R — Read: Read actively to answer your questions. Notes in your own words only.
R — Recite: Close the book and explain it out loud after each section.
R — Review: Write 3 key points at end of each session. Review before the next session.
